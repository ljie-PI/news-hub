# RankSpot - AI SEO Blog driven by deep competitor intelligence

## Product Overview

RankSpot is a fully automated AI SEO agent positioned as "your personal SEO/GEO AI agent working 24/7." Driven by deep competitor intelligence, it automatically researches, writes, and publishes SEO-optimized articles to users' blogs daily, aiming to gain exposure and citations on Google Search and AI answers (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini). The product gained significant attention on Product Hunt in early 2026 and quickly became a hot tool in the AI marketing automation space.

## Core Features

RankSpot's capabilities can be summarized into six automated modules:

1. **Keyword Discovery**: Automatically mines high-intent keywords based on actual user search behavior, ranking them by opportunity.
2. **Article Writing**: Generates 1,500+ word articles daily, dual-optimized for SEO and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).
3. **Auto-Publishing**: After a one-time website connection, articles are automatically published to WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Shopify, Framer, Ghost, and more.
4. **Competitor Monitoring**: Users simply add competitor URLs, and the system automatically tracks their keyword strategies and generates targeted content.
5. **Image Generation**: Creates unique, on-brand images for every article.
6. **Article Optimization**: Automatically handles internal linking, structural optimization, tables, and citation formatting.

Additionally, RankSpot supports 100+ languages, covering major markets such as German, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Chinese, and Hindi, helping businesses acquire customers globally.

RankSpot's key differentiator is "deep competitor intelligence." Traditional SEO tools like Ahrefs and SEMrush provide data but still require manual analysis.
